# RansomForge Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 3, 2026

RansomForge, LLC ("we," "our," or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices when you use RansomForge (the "Service"), available at https://app.ransomforge.com.

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Information You Provide

Depending on how you use the Service, we may collect:
- Email address
- Display name
- Authentication details (for example, password credentials)
- Support or account communications

If you use third-party sign-in (such as Google), we receive basic profile/account identifiers from that provider (for example, a provider user ID, name, and email, subject to provider permissions).

### B. Security and Authentication Data

To secure accounts, we may store:
- Password hashes (not plaintext passwords)
- Multi-factor authentication data (such as TOTP secret status)
- Hashed authentication/verification/reset token records
- Email verification status

### C. Gameplay and Service Data

We may collect data needed to operate gameplay and account features, such as:
- Match outcomes and player ratings
- Queue/party/lobby state
- Account status and region settings

### D. Technical Data

We may automatically collect limited technical information, such as:
- IP address and request metadata
- Approximate usage logs for reliability and abuse prevention

## 2. How We Use Information

We use information to:
- Provide and maintain the Service
- Authenticate users and secure accounts
- Send transactional emails (for example, verification and password reset links)
- Detect, prevent, and investigate abuse, fraud, and security incidents
- Enforce our Terms and legal obligations
- Improve features, stability, and performance

## 3. Legal Bases (Where Required)

Where applicable law requires, we process personal data based on one or more of the following:
- Performance of a contract (providing the Service)
- Legitimate interests (security, fraud prevention, product improvement)
- Compliance with legal obligations
- Consent (where specifically requested)

## 4. How We Share Information

We do not sell personal information.

We may share information:
- With service providers who help us operate the Service (for example, hosting, email delivery, authentication infrastructure)
- If required by law, legal process, or governmental request
- To protect rights, safety, and security of users, us, or others
- In connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or asset transfer

## 5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Policy, including legal, accounting, security, and operational requirements.

Retention may vary by data type. For example, certain authentication tokens are designed to expire automatically, while account records may be retained while your account remains active and for a reasonable period thereafter.

## 6. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 7. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:
- Access personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete personal data
- Restrict or object to certain processing
- Request data portability
- Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@ransomforge.com. We may need to verify your identity before responding.

## 8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13 (or older age where required by local law). If you believe a child provided personal information in violation of this Policy, contact us so we can take appropriate action.

## 9. International Transfers

If you access the Service from outside the country where our systems are located, your information may be transferred to and processed in other jurisdictions that may have different data protection laws.

## 10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If material changes are made, we will post the updated Policy with a new effective date and may provide additional notice where required.
